## Overview

The **PLAUD Client SDK** lets your app capable of control Plaud devices with minimal effort.  
Currently, it is designed for **Android and iOS developers** who need to integrate PLAUD devices into their applications.

<Frame>
  <img src="/assets/sdk-architecture.png" alt="Plaud Client SDKs" />
</Frame>

## Capabilities at a glance

- Connect & manage devices (scan, connect, unbind, disconnect)  
- Retrieve device info (battery, storage, state)  
- Recording control (start, pause, resume, stop)  
- Data export (file listing, resumable downloads, delete/clear)  
- Wi-Fi sync (configure, test, get results)
