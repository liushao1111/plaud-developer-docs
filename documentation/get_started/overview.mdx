## Ways to integrate?
The Developer Platform empowers you to:
1.  **Embed Plaud** directly into your SaaS product offering, providing your users Plaud devices and using Plaud’s ASR, diarization, and AI pipelines on the backend.
2.  **OAuth integration** to pull data and insights from your users' Plaud accounts ([Join Waitlist](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdndvVU1mcRGg_E7YURPEtB13R1zHs55T-234AC6STJyW1n8w/viewform?usp=header))

<Tip>
**Note:**  
This documentation site is currently focused on use case #1 - embedding Plaud into your SaaS product offering. If you are interested in getting integrating with Plaud user accounts via the OAuth API (currently in progress), please fill out [this survey here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdndvVU1mcRGg_E7YURPEtB13R1zHs55T-234AC6STJyW1n8w/viewform) to join the waitlist and we will notify you as soon as it’s available..
</Tip>

## How It Works

<Steps>
<Step title="1. Capture Conversations">
Use our mobile SDK or API to enable users to bind Plaud Devices with your application, enabling them to begin recording and capturing conversational data seamlessly to be used in your application.
</Step>

<Step title="2. Extract Structured Insights">
Plaud handles the end-to-end pipelines for audio pre-processing, transcription, diarization, and AI summarization, and provides tooling so you can output structured JSON to turn that unstructured conversation data into structured insights from every conversation.
</Step>

<Step title="3. Deliver Data Anywhere">
Recieve the processed payload(s) in your application backend, ready to use in your users' subsequen workflows.
</Step>
</Steps>
