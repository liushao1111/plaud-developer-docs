## What can you build?
The Developer Platform empowers you to:
1.  **Enrich your SaaS product** by embedding conversational intelligence for your customers.
2.  **Automate your organization** by building custom workflows from conversational insights.

## How It Works

<Steps>
<Step title="1. Capture Conversations">
Use our SDK or API to connect users with their Plaud Devices, enabling them to begin recording and capturing conversational data seamlessly.
</Step>

<Step title="2. Extract Structured Insights">
Transform raw audio into structured JSON. Define custom schemas and prompts in the Developer Platform to extract the precise data points you need.
</Step>

<Step title="3. Deliver Data Anywhere">
Push the structured data to any destination—your application's backend, a user's CRM, or any other third-party tool—using webhooks or our API.
</Step>
</Steps>
