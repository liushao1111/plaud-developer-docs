# Welcome to Plaud Developer Platform

Plaud.ai provides industry-leading AI recording hardware and a powerful developer platform. With our APIs, you can:

- **Manage Devices:** Register, update, and monitor Plaud recording devices
- **Access Recordings:** List and manage audio files from your devices
- **Transcription & AI Summaries:** Retrieve transcriptions and AI-generated summaries for your recordings
- **User & Tenant Management:** Organize users and tenants for multi-organization support
