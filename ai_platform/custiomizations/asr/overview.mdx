## What is ASR in Plaud AI Platform?

Plaud AI Platform's Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) system is designed to handle complex audio processing scenarios in business environments. Our ASR system combines advanced speech-to-text conversion with intelligent audio segmentation to deliver accurate transcription results.

### Processing Pipeline

The ASR system processes audio through a streamlined pipeline:

1. **VAD**: Voice Activity Detection identifies speech segments and filters out silence
2. **STT**: Speech-to-Text converts audio segments into raw text transcription  
3. **HOTWORD**: Custom vocabulary recognition for technical terms and proper nouns
4. **FORMATTING**: Adds punctuation, capitalization, and proper text formatting

<Note>
This linear pipeline ensures consistent, high-quality transcription results for business audio recordings.
</Note>

- **Multi-language Support**: Process recordings in multiple languages with high accuracy
- **Business-focused**: Optimized for business scenarios and professional conversations
- **Intelligent Processing**: Advanced algorithms for punctuation, formatting, and hotword recognition
