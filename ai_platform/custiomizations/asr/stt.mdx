## Overview

Plaud AI Platform's Speech-to-Text (STT) system converts audio recordings into accurate text transcriptions with advanced language processing capabilities. Our STT engine is specifically optimized for business scenarios and supports multiple languages with intelligent formatting features.

## Core Capabilities

### Multi-language Transcription
- **Language Detection**: Automatically detect the primary language in audio recordings
- **Multi-language Support**: Process recordings containing multiple languages seamlessly
- **Regional Accents**: Handle various accents and dialects within supported languages

### Advanced Text Processing

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Punctuation & Formatting">
  The STT system automatically adds appropriate punctuation marks and formatting to ensure readable transcriptions:
  
  - **Smart Punctuation**: Automatically insert periods, commas, question marks, and exclamation points
  - **Capitalization**: Proper capitalization for sentences and proper nouns
  - **Number Formatting**: Convert spoken numbers to appropriate written format
  
  <Note>
  The punctuation system uses contextual analysis to determine appropriate placement based on speech patterns and pauses.
  </Note>
</Tab>

<Tab title="Hotword Recognition">
  Enhanced recognition for business-specific terminology and custom vocabulary:
  
  - **Custom Vocabulary**: Add industry-specific terms and company names
  - **Technical Terms**: Improved accuracy for technical and professional terminology
  - **Brand Names**: Better recognition of brand names and product terms
  
  <Tip>
  Configure custom hotwords through the platform settings to improve transcription accuracy for your specific use case.
  </Tip>
</Tab>
</Tabs>

## Technical Features

### Accuracy Optimization
- **Noise Reduction**: Advanced algorithms to filter background noise
- **Speaker Adaptation**: Improved accuracy through speaker recognition
- **Context Awareness**: Use conversation context to improve word recognition

### Output Formats
- **Plain Text**: Clean, formatted text output
- **Timestamped Text**: Include timing information for each word or phrase
- **Structured Data**: JSON format with confidence scores and metadata

<Warning>
Processing time may vary based on audio length and quality. For optimal results, ensure clear audio with minimal background noise.
</Warning>

## Best Practices

<Steps>
<Step title="Audio Quality">
  Ensure high-quality audio input for best transcription results:
  - Use clear recording devices
  - Minimize background noise
  - Maintain consistent volume levels
</Step>

<Step title="Language Configuration">
  Configure language settings appropriately:
  - Set primary language for the recording
  - Enable multi-language detection if needed
  - Configure regional dialect preferences
</Step>

<Step title="Custom Vocabulary">
  Add relevant hotwords and terminology:
  - Include company-specific terms
  - Add technical vocabulary for your industry
  - Update hotword lists regularly
</Step>
</Steps>
